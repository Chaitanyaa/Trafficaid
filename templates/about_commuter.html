Commuters, This way please!

Hello there! Traffic Aid,our pet project is here to help you choose your preferred location and time of travel, and gives you the predicted traffic speed.

  • Enter the Origin location
  • Enter the Destination location
  • Specify the date of travel
  • Hit Enter!

You can now see Traffic Aid magically dispalying the predicted traffic speed between your Origin and Destination on your preferred date!

Traffic Aid gives you accurate predictions based on a study conducted by five Master's students for one year. We created this project based on traffic data from PeMS, the Official California DoT website. We also took multiple data that can affect traffic such as incidents en-route and weather severities. On training five years' data with multiple Machine Learning algorithms, we were able to produce accurate predictions for your service. We bring to you real-time predictions, based on our successful models.

You maybe wondering how we are different from Google Maps! While Google Maps can give you estimated time of arrival from Point A to Point B, Maps does not consider multiple data like weather and incident. We have trained our model with a good amount of historical data, making our model super robust!
