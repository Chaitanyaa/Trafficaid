Welcome, Traffic Planners!

Welcome to Traffic Aid, a real-time traffic prediction project done by 5 Master's of Data Analytics students of San Jose State University. Traffic Aid will help you understand city traffic trends, and it will help in identifying various bottle-necks in the system.

In this project, we have developed real-time traffic prediction models, which can give accurate prediction of traffic speed between two points, on a particular date. This model was developed considering multiple factors that affect traffic such as incidents on the route and weather severities.

In planning Smart City projects, our model can be helpful by giving traffic speed trends on each day and time of the week. Smart City planners can also take into account the incident data given by our model, and plan high-incident risk areas accordingly. Such risky locations can be identified, and improvements in design can be implemented.

Our dashboard gives a clear idea of the training data, which comprises of 5 years data from multiple sources. Various models that were implemented and their success rates can be seen on the dashboard. This data can be useful in getting insights about multiple factors that affect traffic speed on various freeways such as Freeway 101, I-680, I-880 and I-280.


To use Traffic Aid:

  • Select a Freeway
  • Enter a station number
  • Enter Input date
  • Hit Submit and recieve accurate analysis of travel conditions!
